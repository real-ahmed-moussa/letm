| Entity | character | "Russia" | Country name |

| Life_Expectancy | numeric | 16.00–70.87 | Period life expectancy at birth (years) |

## Notable Observations

- **1943**: Minimum of 16.00 years — wartime famine and WWII casualties
- **2016**: Maximum of 70.87 years — modern healthcare and stability
- **Structural phases**: pre-industrial stability (~1800–1850), turbulent non-monotone growth with wartime disruptions (~1850–1960), late-century plateau (~1960–2016)
